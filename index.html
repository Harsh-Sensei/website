About Me

I am a graduate student pursuing Master's in Machine Learning program at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). I love to involve myself with complex and interesting systems, and autonomous robots are no different.

Currently, I have been working in Auton Lab (advised by Jeff Schneider) with the self-driving group to improve language conditioned autonomous driving via evolutionary search on diffusion policies (more info here). (Demo).

Earlier, I use to lead Mars Rover Team at IITB, where we used make autonomous rovers to rule over unknown terrains.
